1. What is Responsible Gaming?

Responsible gaming means enjoying games in a way that is safe, controlled, and doesn't negatively impact your life, relationships, or finances. It involves:

  • Setting time limits for gaming activities
  • Never spending more money than you can afford to lose
  • Not using gaming as a way to escape problems or negative emotions
  • Maintaining a healthy balance between gaming and other life activities
  • Recognizing when gaming becomes problematic and seeking help
